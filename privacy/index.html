Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use PhyloGuessr. Google Analytics collects information such as how often you visit, what pages you view, and your approximate location. This data is aggregated and anonymous. Google Analytics places cookies on your browser to identify you as a unique visitor. For more details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Account and score data

If you choose to log in, we use Firebase Authentication to manage your account. We store your game scores in Firebase so that you can track your history and appear on the leaderboard. We only store what's needed to make those features work: your display name, your scores, and a user identifier.

We also use Firebase Realtime Database to track anonymous presence data (e.g. how many people are currently online). This happens for all visitors, not just logged-in users, and does not include any personally identifiable information.

Cookies

We use cookies for Google Analytics (as described above) and to keep you logged in if you have an account.

Third-party services

We rely on Google Analytics and Firebase (both Google services). Their use of your data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

If we change this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
